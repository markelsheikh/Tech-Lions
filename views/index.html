The Tech lions College School Registration System is a registration system used by students, faculty members and administrators. Students will be able connect to the system securely and register for courses. Faculty will be able to manage their classes, and administrators will be able to create class schedules. Tech lions will provide a safe connection to all of its users.

what Techlions College does

With a user friendly and secure system, all of it's users will easily be able to complete their tasks.

  • Administration

    Techlions will allow the administration to easily perform their functionality such as creating departments, courses, and course sections. administrators will be also able add/remove a hold on a students account.

  • Faculty

    Our innovative registration system will allow professors to assign grades, take attendance, and see their student advisement list .

  • Researhcer

    Researchers at Techlions college will be able to research students information and faculty's information. this will allow researchers to find issues in the academic practices at Techlions college. By the data provieded it will allow researchers to hand out advice to administrators so they can make decisions.

  • Students

    Students have the greatest benefit in this innovative registration system. they will be able to view their entire academic career through Techlions. Students will be able to add a course or drop a course. they can view their schedule, their adviser, and their grades.
